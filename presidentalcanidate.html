Trump vs Hillary

The 2016 presidential election is one for the history books. The Republican party candidate is Donald Trump and the Democratic party candidate is Hillary Clinton. As of October 4th, 2016, Clinton is in the lead, but will she stay there? Stay up to date with the election, but before you vote, know who you're voting for.



Hillary Clinton

  1. Born on October 26, 1947 in Chicago Illinois
  2. Hillary attended Wellesley College and Yale Law School, where she met her husband. After she completed her law degree, she also took postgraduate classes at Yale Child Study Center.
  3. She served as the 67th U.S. secretary of state during Obama first term in office and she was the third female secretary of state in U.S. history.
  4. Before politics, she had dreams of becoming a baseball player, a journalist, or an astronaut.
  5. She won a Grammy in 1997 for Best Spoken Word Album for the audio version of her book It Takes a Village.

Donald Trump

  1. Trump was born on June 14, 1946 in New York, New York
  2. Trump considered running for president in 2000 under the Reform party but didnt.
  3. He went to the University of Pennsylvania Wharton School of Finance.
  4. In January 2004, he launched his TV show, The Apprentice. The name was later changed to Celebrity Apprentice.
  5. He has 5 children. With Marla: Tiffany Trump. With Melania: Barron Trump. With Ivana: Eric Trump, Ivanka Trump, and Donald Trump Jr.
